3. Data We Collect

3.1 Data We Do NOT Collect

Focus Together does NOT collect:
  • Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • Your focus session data or statistics
  • Your achievements, XP, or level progress
  • Your timer settings or preferences
  • Any user-generated content

All of this data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

3.2 Data That May Be Collected by Third Parties

While we do not collect your data, our advertising partner (Google AdMob) may collect certain information to serve advertisements. See Section 5 for details.

4. Local Data Storage

4.1 What Is Stored Locally

The following data is stored on your device only:

Data Type Description Purpose
User Profile Display name, daily goal, creation date Personalization
Focus Sessions Session duration, type, completion status, timestamps Statistics tracking
Daily Logs Minutes focused, sessions completed, goal achieved per day Streak and goal tracking
Statistics Total focus time, completed sessions, current streak, longest streak Progress tracking
Gamification XP, level, unlocked achievements, completed challenges Gamification features
Settings Timer durations, sound preferences, notification settings, theme App customization

4.2 How Local Storage Works

Important: Your data is stored using your device's local storage (localStorage). This means:
  • Data stays on your device and is never uploaded to any server
  • Data persists between app sessions
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all data
  • There is no cloud backup or sync functionality
  • We cannot recover your data if it is lost

4.3 Deleting Your Local Data

You can delete all your local data at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset All Data" option in Profile → Settings within the app
  • Uninstalling the app from your device
  • Clearing app data through your device's settings

Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

5. Advertisements and Third-Party Data Collection

5.1 Google AdMob

Focus Together is a free app supported by advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob is a third-party advertising service operated by Google LLC.

5.2 Ad Frequency

To ensure a positive user experience, we limit the frequency of advertisements:

  • Interstitial Ads: Displayed at most once every 12 hours when navigating to the Statistics screen
  • Advertisements are shown at natural transition points and do not interrupt active focus sessions

5.3 App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS devices, Focus Together requests your permission to track your activity through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. This is required by Apple for apps that display personalized advertisements.

  • If you allow tracking: Google AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to show you more relevant advertisements
  • If you deny tracking: You will see general (non-personalized) advertisements

Your choice does not affect the frequency of ads or access to any app features. You can change your choice at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

5.4 Data Collected by AdMob

Google AdMob may collect certain information to serve advertisements:

Data Type Purpose
Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS) Ad personalization and measurement (if you allow tracking)
Device Information Device type, OS version, screen size
IP Address Approximate location for relevant ads
Ad Interaction Data Clicks, views, and engagement metrics

5.5 Managing Ad Personalization

You can control how Google uses your data for advertising:

  • iOS ATT: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Focus Together
  • iOS Ad Settings: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Turn off "Personalized Ads"
  • Google Ad Settings: Visit adssettings.google.com to manage your Google ad preferences

5.6 Google's Privacy Policy

Google's data collection through AdMob is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google Uses Information from Apps
  • AdMob Data Collection
Note: We do not have access to the data collected by Google AdMob. This data is processed entirely by Google according to their privacy policy.

8. Your Rights

Under the GDPR and German data protection law (BDSG), you have the following rights:

8.1 Right of Access (Art. 15 GDPR)

You have the right to know what data we collect. Since all your Focus Together data is stored locally on your device, you have complete access to it at all times through the app.

8.2 Right to Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)

You can delete all your data at any time using the "Reset All Data" feature in the app's Profile settings, or by uninstalling the app.

8.3 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20 GDPR)

Your data is stored in standard formats on your device. You have direct access to this data.

8.4 Rights Regarding Advertising

For data collected by Google AdMob:

  • You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings
  • You can manage your Google ad preferences at adssettings.google.com
  • You can contact Google directly regarding your advertising data
