10. Your Rights

Under the GDPR and German data protection law (BDSG), you have the following rights:

10.1 Right of Access (Art. 15 GDPR)

You have the right to obtain confirmation of whether we process your personal data and, if so, access to that data along with information about how it is processed.

10.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)

You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal data and completion of incomplete personal data. You can update most information directly in the App settings.

10.3 Right to Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data. You can delete your account directly in the App (Profile → Settings → Delete Account), which will remove your data as described above.

10.4 Right to Restriction (Art. 18 GDPR)

You have the right to request restriction of processing of your personal data in certain circumstances, such as when you contest the accuracy of the data.

10.5 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20 GDPR)

You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format. Premium subscribers can export their focus data through the App.

10.6 Right to Object (Art. 21 GDPR)

You have the right to object to processing of your personal data based on legitimate interests. If you object, we will no longer process your data unless we demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds.

10.7 Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3) GDPR)

Where we rely on consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before the withdrawal.

10.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. In Baden-Württemberg, Germany, the relevant authority is:

11. Data Security

11.1 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

Technical Measures

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Encryption at Rest: Data stored in Firebase is encrypted at rest
  • Secure Authentication: We use OAuth 2.0 through Google and Apple Sign-In
  • Password Hashing: Room passwords are hashed before storage
  • Access Controls: Firebase Security Rules restrict data access to authorized users

Organizational Measures

  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security reviews and updates
  • Incident response procedures

11.2 Content Moderation

To maintain a safe environment, we implement:

  • Automated profanity and spam filtering
  • Rate limiting on messages and actions
  • Input sanitization to prevent malicious content
  • Restrictions on sharing personal information in chats

11.3 Your Responsibilities

You can help protect your data by:

  • Using strong, unique passwords for your Google or Apple account
  • Enabling two-factor authentication on your sign-in provider
  • Not sharing room passwords with untrusted individuals
  • Not sharing sensitive information in Focus Room chats
  • Keeping your device and operating system updated

11.4 Security Incidents

In the event of a data breach that poses a high risk to your rights and freedoms, we will:

  • Notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours
  • Notify affected users without undue delay
  • Take immediate steps to mitigate the impact
